ScoutLog

An app that helps you save important locations along with photos and notes
Features:
  • Save a location with a title, notes, tags, and photos
  • View your saved locations in a list, grid or map
  • Search your locations by text or tags
  • Sync your locations across any number of Android devices using a Google Drive account
  • Export your locations to a zip file for sharing
  • Light and dark themes
Requires Android 4.0+
